Biography

I am an undergraduate student at Chu Kochen Honors College, Zhejiang University. In July 2022, I will graduate from the Interdisciplinary Honors Program of automation and mechatronic engineering. During my undergraduate study, I have worked as a member in ZJU robotics lab, under the supervision of Prof. Yue Wang and Prof. Rong Xiong. Additionally, I have participated a research project in Neural Robotics lab and worked with Prof. Qinyuan Ren. Currently, I’m conducting research of my summer internship program in CUHK, under the supervision of Prof. Qi Dou.

My research interests lie in robotics, machine learning and computer vision, especially the applications of CV in the domain of perception in robotics, including following topics in detail:

  1. Multi-modal learning algorithms to intergrate different data.
  2. Reinforcement learning for computer vision

Motivated by the strong interests in robotics and computer vision, I’m determined to continue conducting researches among these fields in the future.
